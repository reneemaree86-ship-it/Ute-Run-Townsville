# Data Deletion Policy

## How to Request Account and Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their Ute-Run Townsville account and associated personal data through one of the following methods:

### Email Request (Manual Process)

Send an email to: **uteruntownsville@gmail.com**

Please include:
- Email address used in the app, OR
- Phone number used in the app

### Web Form Request

You can also submit a data deletion request using our [Data Deletion Form](https://ute-runtownsville.online/data-deletion/)

## Processing Timeline

Data deletion requests are reviewed and actioned as soon as reasonably possible. You will receive a confirmation email once your request has been processed.

## What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following personal data will be removed:
- Account profile information (name, email, phone number)
- Account preferences and settings
- Booking history and associated records
- Payment information
- Any other personally identifiable information

Some data may be retained for:
- Legal compliance and regulatory requirements
- Dispute resolution and fraud prevention
- Limited periods as required by law

## Questions?

If you have any questions about our data deletion process, please contact us at uteruntownsville@gmail.com
