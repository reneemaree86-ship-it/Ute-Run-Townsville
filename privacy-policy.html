Privacy Policy

UteRun Townsville Pty Ltd ("we", "us", "our") operates the UteRun Townsville platform that connects customers with independent driver contractors for pickups, deliveries, and small moves in the Townsville region.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: Name, email address, phone number, password (hashed).

Driver information: Driver's licence details, vehicle details, ABN (for businesses), bank/payout details (processed via Stripe).

Usage data: Job history, messages between users, ratings and reviews, location data (during active jobs only).

Device data: IP address, device type, app version.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match customers with drivers
  • To process payments via Stripe
  • To provide live job tracking (location shared only during active jobs)
  • To send transactional emails and SMS (OTP verification, job updates)
  • To maintain safety and resolve disputes

3. Information Sharing

We share information only as necessary to operate the platform:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (card details never stored on our servers)
  • Twilio: SMS verification codes
  • Google Maps: Location and mapping services
  • Between users: Customer and driver see each other's name, phone, and job details for active jobs only

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored in secure cloud databases with encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. Access is restricted and logged. JWT tokens expire after 7 days with refresh token rotation.

5. Location Data

Location is collected only during active jobs with your permission. Drivers share their live location so customers can track deliveries. Location sharing stops when the job is completed.

6. Your Rights

Under Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, email support@uterun.com.au

7. Data Retention

We retain account data while your account is active. Transaction records are kept for 7 years as required by Australian tax law. Deleted account data is purged within 30 days except where legally required.

8. Children's Privacy

UteRun is for users 18 years and older only. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
